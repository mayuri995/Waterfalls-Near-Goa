All about Jalavne Waterfall


Jalavane Waterfalls, a hidden gem on the Goa-Karnataka border, is a picturesque cascade known for its serene beauty and lush surroundings. It's a popular spot for nature lovers and trekkers, offering a peaceful escape into the Sahyadri hills. The waterfall is accessible from both Goa and Karnataka, specifically from Belagavi, and involves a challenging trek through forests.

Key Features:

Location:

Situated on the Goa-Karnataka border, near Belagavi (Balagavi).

Accessibility:

Can be reached from both Goa and Karnataka, specifically Surla village near Belagavi.

Trekking:

Involves a trek through the forest, which can be challenging, particularly for novice hikers.

Surroundings:

Enveloped by dense forests, offering a tranquil and immersive experience with nature.

Crystal-clear pool:

The water falling from the falls creates a pool at the bottom, surrounded by rocks and vegetation.

Hidden gem:

Potential Restrictions:

There are reports of the waterfall being closed and restricted. Additionally, some users mention that it is located within a protected area and trespassing could lead to legal consequences.
