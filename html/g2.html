All about Green Valley Waterfall

Green Valley Waterfall, also known as Asla Waterfall, is a popular destination located near the Goa-Maharashtra border, specifically in the Tilari Nagar area. It's known for its scenic beauty and the trek required to reach it, which involves descending around 2-2.5 km from the Green Valley Resort. The waterfall is situated 48km away from Belagavi and is surrounded by lush greenery, making it a popular spot for nature lovers and photographers.

Here's a more detailed look:

Location:

The waterfall is part of the Green Valley Resort area, near the Goa-Maharashtra border in Tilari Nagar.

Accessibility:

It's not directly accessible by road; you need to trek down from the Green Valley Resort, which involves a 30-minute hike through a forest area.

Trekking Difficulty:

The trek is considered easy to moderate, but it's important to be careful, especially during or after rainfall, as the path can be slippery.

Unique Feature:

The waterfall has a unique hollow space beneath the large rock from which it falls.

Swimming:

The water is deep enough for swimming, but caution is advised, especially near the falls where it can be deeper.

Best Time to Visit:

The best time to visit is typically between August and December.

Nearby Attractions:

Green Valley Waterfall is near the Tilari waterfalls and can be visited together.
