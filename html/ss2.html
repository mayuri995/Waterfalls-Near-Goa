All about Swapnwell Waterfall

Swapnavel Waterfall, also known as Swapnavel Point, is a scenic viewpoint and waterfall located in the Western Ghats, specifically near Chorla Ghat in Maharashtra. It's known for its beautiful views of mountain ridges and waterfalls, making it a popular spot for nature lovers and those seeking a tranquil escape. The area is surrounded by lush greenery, hills, and valleys.

Key features of Swapnavel Waterfall:

Scenic Beauty:

The location offers breathtaking panoramic views of the surrounding landscape, including the waterfalls and mountain ridges.

Monsoon Magic:

The waterfall is particularly impressive during the monsoon season (typically August-September), when the area is lush and the waterfalls are at their fullest.

Surrounding Area:

It's surrounded by dense forests, making it a great place for nature walks and spotting diverse plant and animal life.

Swapnavel Resort:

There's a resort in the area, Swapnavel Resort, which offers accommodations and is designed to be eco-conscious, allowing guests to immerse themselves in the natural environment.

Potential Concerns:

While beautiful, some reviews mention issues with the resort's maintenance and cleanliness, as well as the condition of the access road.
