All about Sundi

Sundi Waterfall: Drive of 25 km from Belagavi - Namma ... Sundi Waterfall is a famous waterfall in Chandgad taluka of Kolhapur district. The waterfall is very famous among the tourists and is also known as the "Constitution Water".

More information about Sundi Waterfall:

Location:

The Sundi waterfall is in Chandigarh taluka of Kolhapur district.

The nearest village:

The waterfall is on the Amboli-Belgaum route and is about 15 kilometers from Amboli.

Attraction:

The Sundi waterfall gives tourists a calm and beautiful experience in nature. Tourists love the green shrubs and waterfalls here.
