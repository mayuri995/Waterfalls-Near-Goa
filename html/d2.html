All about Dhangarmola Waterfall

Dhangarmola Waterfall is a tourist attraction located in Suleran, Kolhapur, Maharashtra, India. It's known for its scenic beauty and is a popular spot for nature lovers and those seeking adventure. The waterfall is characterized by its multi-tiered structure, with water cascading down rocky steps. It's particularly impressive during the monsoon season (June to September) when the water flow is at its peak.

Key Features:

Location:

Suleran, Kolhapur, Maharashtra.

Natural Beauty:

Surrounded by lush greenery and rocky terrain, offering a serene environment.

Waterfall Characteristics:

Multi-tiered with varying flow based on the season.

Activities:

Trekking, nature walks, and swimming in natural pools are popular activities.

Best Time to Visit:

Monsoon season (June to September) for dramatic water flow.

Nearby Attractions:

While the primary attraction is the waterfall itself, the surrounding area offers opportunities for exploration and enjoying the natural beauty of the Western Ghats.

Treks and Trails India also mentions rappelling and flying fox activities at a similar-sounding "Dhangar Waterfall" near Badlapur, which is another location near Mumbai, but this may be a separate location from the one in Kolhapur.

Visitor Information:

  • No tickets are required to visit the waterfall.
  • Free admission.
  • Consider visiting during the monsoon season for the best experience.
