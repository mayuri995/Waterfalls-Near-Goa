All about Rautwadi Waterfall

Rautwadi Waterfall, located near Radhanagari in Kolhapur, Maharashtra, is a popular tourist attraction known for its scenic beauty and refreshing waters, especially during the monsoon season. It's a relatively easy and safe place to visit, offering a relaxing experience amidst lush greenery.

Key Features and Information:

Location:

Rautwadi Waterfall is situated in Bhogavati, Kolhapur, and is about an hour's drive from Kolhapur city. It's approximately 10 km from Radhanagari town.

Monsoon Beauty:

The waterfall is particularly stunning during the monsoon (July-September), with a strong water flow.

Accessibility:

The approach to the waterfall is considered easy and safe, making it suitable for families and those seeking a less strenuous outing.

Scenic Surroundings:

The waterfall is surrounded by lush greenery, providing picturesque views.

Facilities:

While some reviews mention a lack of amenities like changing rooms and washrooms, others note the presence of changing rooms for ladies and clean toilets, enhancing visitor comfort.

Activities:

Visitors can enjoy the cool water, take photos, and explore the area around the waterfall. Some reviews mention enjoying hot corn and other snacks at nearby stalls.

Crowd:

While it can be a popular spot, it's often not overly crowded, especially on weekdays, offering a more peaceful experience.

Safety:

It's important to be cautious near the waterfall, especially during the monsoon, as landslides can occur.

Best Time to Visit:

The monsoon season (July-September) is the ideal time to witness the waterfall's full glory.

Nearby Attractions:

Besides the waterfall, visitors can explore nearby attractions like the Private Aguada Jail Heritage Walk Tour, Historical Tours, and Olavan, Kolhapur.
