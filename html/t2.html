all about tilhari

Tillari is a beautiful place in Chandgad taluka, which is a part of Kolhapur district in Maharashtra. This place is famous for the Tillari River and the Tillari Dam. Information about Tillari:

    Tilari River: The Tilari River originates in the Sahyadri Mountains and later joins the Arabian Sea in Goa.
    Tilari Dam: A large dam has been built on the Tilari River near the village of Dhamne, which is called the Tilari Dam.
    Tilari Ghat: Tilari Ghat is located in Chandgad taluka and is surrounded by dense forest. Various types of wildlife are found in this area.
    Biodiversity: The area of ​​Tilari is rich in biodiversity. Many types of plants and animals are found here.
    Chandgad Taluka:
  • Chandgad is a taluka of Kolhapur district.
  • This taluka is located at the very southern tip of Maharashtra.
  • The dialect here is known as 'Chandgadi'.
  • There are many mountains and rivers here.
  • Tilari Ghat, Amboli Ghat and some parts of Ajra taluka in Chandgad taluka are under the influence of the 'Konkani' dialect.
  • In the eastern part of Chandigarh, a dialect influenced by the Kannada language is spoken.
